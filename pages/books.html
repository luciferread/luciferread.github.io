BOOKS

THE RADIANTS

AVAILABLE SOON

On a world that has never known night, the coming eclipse will spark a fight for survival — and expose the truth buried in its own shadow.

Their sun had never set - until now. When the Mintaka discovers a moon orbiting their world, it may spell their doom: an eclipse that will last for millennia. But when their warnings are met with resistance, it becomes clear that the greatest threat to their survival may not come from above.
As rival families turn against one another, old loyalties fracture and new alliances form. A reluctant teacher and a young lumen are drawn into the ancient currents of a world that has forgotten how to face the dark.
As the shadow draws near, who will stand beneath the fading light - and who will survive to see what waits beyond?
For readers of Frank Herbert and N. K. Jemisin, The Radiants is the first book in the Heliopaides saga - an epic tale of a civilisation built on sunlight, and the darkness waiting to reclaim it.
