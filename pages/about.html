ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Lucifer Read is a science fiction author crafting epic, planet-scale stories of extinction, survival, and the consequences of immortality. Inspired by the vastness of space and the mysteries of alien civilizations, their work has been described as "fresh and unique" by early readers, particularly for those looking for new worlds to explore.

When not writing, Lucifer prefers to remain in the light — and out of view.

OFFICIAL BIOS

Short Bio (50 words)

Lucifer Read writes science fiction that explores extinction, endurance, and the consequences of living too long. The Radiants is their debut novel and the first book in the Heliopaides saga, a series examining survival on worlds shaped by unending light.

Long Bio (100 words)

Lucifer Read writes science fiction that examines survival at a civilizational scale, with a particular focus on longevity, immortality, and the tension between endurance and stagnation. Their work is shaped by alien worlds, vast timescales, and societies forced to confront the consequences of existing beyond their natural limits.
The Radiants is Lucifer’s debut novel and the first book in the Heliopaides saga, a multi-volume epic exploring what happens when extinction is delayed rather than avoided — and whether a civilization can endure forever without losing itself.
